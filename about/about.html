About us

Purpose of RealCousins

At Realcousins we understand that learning about your family history is important and can reveal many surprising things that you didn’t know. You can either do a genealogy search online or sit down with members of your family and listen to their stories. No matter how you’ve chosen to gather the information, you’ll end up having a deeper appreciation for who you are and where you come from. Nothing is more eye-opening than learning about who you are...

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect and strengthen family ties through shared values, culture, history and to establish the roots of their identity
